Business Incubation Center

 

A state-of-the-art incubation center provides business facilities and reinforces Iqra University’s philosophy of brining to corporate world, students of the future, with an enterprising mindset. In addition to startups, we want to promote ideas that are scalable and may lead to changing human lives.

Our Mission

Empower to create

To foster an entrepreneurial culture, by providing young student and pioneers opportunities to transform their business ideas into reality.

Performance to true potential

To provide an encouraging working environment to the incubator companies to nurture their innovative ideas.

Catalyst for collaborations

To liaise with private and public sector funding sources, government agencies, industrial associations, and other sectors in order to provide facilitation and networking for incubate companies.
